Slade Wilson: The Loveable Anit-Hero of Maltese Mix Dogs

Slade Wilson is a lovable 2 year old Maltese Mix. He will occassionally embody his namesake. When he embodies his namesake his owners sometimes wonder why they named him Slade Wilson. It is as if his name called for some of the sneaky behavior Slade often has. His owners have taken to calling him Slade, and uses his full name Slade Wilson when he is in trouble.

Slade Wilson: An Anti-Hero in the DC Universe

Slade Wilson is an infamous character in the comics from the DC universe. In 2012 a TV series called Arrow created an adaption of the comic book character, Slade Wilson. The dog Slade Wilson was named after the anti-hero.

Slade Wilson: The Dog

Slade Wilson is a dog of Many Hobbies

One of his favorite hobbies is playing with his maltese friend named Loki. Together they have exciting adventures. They renact the battle scene between Scar and Simba in the Disney Movie The Lion King. The two dogs are often seen running together and playing the epic game of follow the leader.

Another hobby of Slade is swimming in the pool. Slade has no fear of water and will jump into any body of water. He especially loves chasing geese into lakes. He loves to swim in the pool at his grandparents house. However, he recently needed a life jacket. His owners say it is to keep him from drowning. But, he knows it is really to keep him from fitting through the privacy fence by the pool and preventing him from chasing squirrels while his owners are swimming.

His last hobby is snuggling on the couch with his owners. He really enjoys to cuddle with his owners while they watch TV, provided his is given plenty of pets.
